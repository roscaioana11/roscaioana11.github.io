Flappy Kiwi

  • Tech Stack: C#, Unity, Photoshop
  • Game URL: Project Link

Flappy Kiwi is an android phone game.

This game was developed in collaboration with my friends.

Flappy Kiwi is a simple game where the player has to guide the kiwi bird by tapping through a level filled with trees with only a small gap to pass. It's an endless type of game, the goal is to get a high score, and don't let the Kiwi die!
