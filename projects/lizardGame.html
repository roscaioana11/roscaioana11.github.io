DMZ the Lizard

Game made for the ludum dare 46!

  • Tech Stack: C#, Unity, Photoshop
  • Game URL: Project Link

This game was my first interaction in writing code. The game is a simple one, and it's made in collaboration with some of my friends, our goal was to have fun, interact as a team and for me to see what coding means.


You recently bought a new terrarium for DMZ!

Now you need to set up his new home, so he's got a correct temperature, humidity and everything to keep him happy!

But keep in mind to not surcharge the terrarium and not stress him too much.
